   5	**Goal:** Expand `Weapons.kt` from 1 weapon (Longsword) to 38 — 34 registered + 4 vanilla-mapped role specs — and ship the corresponding crafting recipes, language strings, item models, vanilla role tags, and 16x16 textures (AI-generated via the `minecraft-asset-generator` agent) across all 5 MC versions.
   6	
   7	**Architecture:** Append 37 `val` declarations to the `Weapons` Kotlin `object` (data-only edit; `WeaponRegistrarImpl` already handles `isVanillaMapped` via early-return). For each of 33 newly-registered weapons, ship one shared recipe JSON (1.20.5+ schema) under `data/dndweapons/recipe/<id>.json` plus a 1.20.1-flavored overlay under `versions/1.20.1/src/main/resources/data/dndweapons/recipes/<id>.json` (plural folder + `"item"` result key), one model JSON, and one Gemini-generated 16x16 PNG texture. The 4 vanilla-mapped specs ship only a `WeaponSpec` entry + role tag JSON + lang entry — no recipe/model/texture, since they piggyback on vanilla items. `WeaponsTest.kt` expands to assert the full-catalog invariants.
